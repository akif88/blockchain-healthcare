Healthcare Blockchain System

The crucial point of the health care system we have been providing is the privacy and integrity of patient records.

It is also necessary for data scientists who wish to make inferences about any disease using patient records to have anonymous access to these data. To achieve this we use blockchain technology. Blockchain is a linked list or distributed database that uses cryptography. The initial aim of the blockchain is to make digital money transfers between two people securely and openly without a third authority. Everyone who participates in the system can see clearly what actions they have done. In the health care system, information about what kind of procedures patients are going to and which hospital they are going to be kept. It is also important to design a system that allows the patient to see only the patient's information, such as the medical information and which doctor is being treated, as well as the third party that the patient and the patient are allowed to see. The treating doctor can determine what information the patient wants to share. This is to prevent someone else from seeing their personal notes that the doctor does not want to share.

MedRec

MedRec is a healthcare blockchain application on which we base. Medrec application MIT Media Lab. was first developed by researchers in 2016 and white paper was published. There are three main elements in MedRec. These are patient, provider and medical stakeholder (researcher, public health authorities, etc.). Patients have a comprehensive and unchanging record and easy access to providers. Providers can integrate with existing local databases. Medical stakeholders are encouraged to join the blockchain network as miners. They are given collective and anonymous data as a reward.
